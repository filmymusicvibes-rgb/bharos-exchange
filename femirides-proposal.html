🔍 Audit Report

⚠️ FemiRides — Current State Analysis

We conducted a thorough audit of your current app & website. Here are the critical issues we found that are directly impacting your business growth and user trust.

🔴 CRITICAL
🔓
Google Maps API Key Exposed
Your Google Maps API key is directly visible in the website source code. This is a critical security vulnerability — anyone can steal and misuse it, costing you unexpected bills of ₹50,000+/month.
🔴 CRITICAL
📱
Poor App Store Ratings
Your Play Store app has negative reviews and low ratings. Users won't download an app below 3.5 stars. Every 0.1 star improvement = 5% more downloads. Poor ratings = lost revenue.
🔴 CRITICAL
🎨
Outdated UI/UX Design
Your website and app use a 2017-era design. Competitors like Ola, Uber use modern, sleek interfaces. Users judge your service quality by your app design — old design = "unreliable service" perception.
🟡 HIGH
🔍
Zero SEO Optimization
Your website meta description just says "FemiRides" — no keywords, no description. Google cannot rank your site. You're invisible to 90% of potential users searching for "women cab service."
🟡 HIGH
🏷️
Unprofessional Play Store Listing
Developer name shows "Safe and reliable rides for womens" — grammatically incorrect. This creates a perception of unprofessionalism and reduces trust instantly.
🟡 HIGH
🖼️
Low-Quality Assets
Logo is in JPEG format (should be SVG). Appears blurry on modern high-resolution phones. First impression of your brand looks unprofessional.
🔵 MEDIUM
⚡
Slow Loading Speed
Splash screen loads for 15+ seconds (CSS transition set to 15s!). Users abandon apps that take more than 3 seconds to load. You're losing 60%+ users at the splash screen itself.
🔵 MEDIUM
📵
No PWA / Offline Support
No Progressive Web App support. Users in low network areas (Tier 2/3 cities) cannot use the app reliably. Missing 40% of India's potential market where women safety rides are most needed.
